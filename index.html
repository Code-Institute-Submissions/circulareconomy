Circular Action
Refuse Rethink Reduce Reuse Repair Refurbish Reporpouse Recycle

reusing, reparing or sharing are examples of circular actions that help to build circular systems by SLOWING AND CLOSING LOOPS of materials and products minimasing resources input and waste

REFUSE - RETHINK

Make overconsumption unappealing and unnecessary, reduce redundancy by offering the same function with a radically innovative design Make product use more intensive with multifunctional products, rethinking ownership and long-lasting design

REDUCE

Phase-out the use of non-renewable resources throughout the supply chain, while increasing efficiency in product manufacturing, distribution and use through reducing the use of new raw material and harmful emissions

REUSE

Extend usage time by one or several users until the product or material reaches the end of its service life until its condition allow to fulfil the original function

REPAIR - REFURBISH

Fornitura e installazione di vetri, cristalli, specchi su misura per interni o esterni, telai per finestre in alluminio, PVC o ferro, tapparelle, persiane, serrande e cancelli di varie tipologie

REMANUFACTURE - REPURPOSE

Use materials from a discarded product for making new product that have the same function Use a product or its part for a new product with a different function

RECYCLE

Process material from a discarded product that in part can be used to create a new product with a function that has a lower or same quality, also downcycling
